It's sunday morning. You're planning to have lunch with your kids and have no idea where to go. Maybe you are on vacation in a strange city, maybe you just want to try something new. With your help We are going to develop a network from parents for parents. A place where we could share tips of great places to go with children of any age.

We'll be a mobile first social network of parents with geolocation support that will find places to go around you location. Every place can and will be ranked by our social tools, so you can choose the best place by age, services, etc.

FAQ

Why should I back this project?
Backing this project you'll make the life or parents everywhere in the world easier by provinding a focused tool to help them share and learn about great places to go with their children. You also will receive founder's benefits.

Founder's benefits are:
  • Never bother with ads - If we ever implement ads, founders will have the option to disable them.
  • Free tiers - If we ever implement paid tiers, the highest will be made free for our founders.
